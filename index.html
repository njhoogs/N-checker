Soil temp interpolated to 10cm from Open-Meteo's 6cm/18cm modelled layers, using the 9am reading each day. Rain forecast is summed over the next 5 days from yr.no (MET Norway) where the browser can reach it, falling back to Open-Meteo's ECMWF model otherwise. Trigger: 10cm temp ≥6°C and trending up over the last 5 days, with <40mm forecast over the next 5 days. Modelled data — treat as a guide alongside what you see and feel in the paddock.
