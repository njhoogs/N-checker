Spring N Trigger

Tap or drag the pin to set the exact location.

Soil temp interpolated to 10cm from Open-Meteo's 6cm/18cm modelled layers, using the daily mean (24h average) rather than a single reading. Growth-start trigger follows Frame's rule: ryegrass begins growing once soil temp holds ≥5.5°C for 5 consecutive days. Accumulated growing degree days (base 5°C, from 1 June) are shown as a secondary indicator of growth strength — the 20kgDM/ha/day threshold is a starting estimate that should be calibrated against your own farm-walk data over a season. Rain forecast is summed over the next 7 days from yr.no (MET Norway) where the browser can reach it, falling back to Open-Meteo's ECMWF model otherwise. Modelled data — treat as a guide alongside what you see and feel in the paddock.
